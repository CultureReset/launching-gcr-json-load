🏆 Year of Alabama Food Award
Their Banana Leaf Wrapped Fish was named one of "the top 100 dishes to eat before you die."
Off the beaten path on Canal Road in Orange Beach, Cosmo's Restaurant & Bar is far from ordinary. Serving fresh and eclectic food since day one, Cosmo's features a wide range of dining, enticing even the most discriminate palate. Using chef-inspired ingredients, you can enjoy everything from hand-cut steaks to local seafood and pasta.

This neighborhood favorite on the Gulf Coast has taken cuisine to the cutting edge. Culinary greats such as the chicken roulade, sesame seared tuna salad, and traditional crab cakes done with a twist will have you wanting more.
